# Grow Simulator — Core Game Balance

This document defines the canonical gameplay parameters used by all agents.

These values must remain consistent across the simulation engine, gameplay systems, and events.

---

# 1. Lifecycle Duration

The entire plant lifecycle is compressed into:

REAL TIME: ~7 days

SIMULATION TIME: ~56 days

Compression factor:

1 real day = 8 simulation days

This allows a realistic lifecycle without long waiting times.

---

# 2. Simulation Time Model

Recommended base speed:

1 real minute = 5 simulation minutes

Simulation continues while the app is closed using timestamp delta calculations.

---

# 3. Day / Night Cycle

Simulation hours:

Daytime (active gameplay):  
08:00 → 22:00

Nighttime:  
22:00 → 08:00

Rules:

Nighttime:
- No gameplay events
- Passive plant metabolism only

Daytime:
- Events may occur
- Player actions allowed

---

# 4. Growth Stages

12 growth stages:

1 Germination  
2 Seedling  
3 Early Vegetative  
4 Vegetative  
5 Late Vegetative  
6 Pre-flower  
7 Stretch  
8 Early Flower  
9 Flower  
10 Late Flower  
11 Ripening  
12 Harvest Ready

Stage transitions depend on:

- simulation time
- plant health
- stress level

Poor plant care delays stage transitions.

---

# 5. Core Plant State Variables

Each plant must maintain the following state values:

water  
nutrition  
health  
growth  
stress  
risk

Range recommendation:

0 → 100

Meaning:

water → hydration level  
nutrition → nutrient availability  
health → overall vitality  
growth → stage progression modifier  
stress → negative environmental impact  
risk → probability of negative events

---

# 6. Player Action Categories

Care system must include:

Watering  
Fertilizing  
Training  
Environment Control

Each category has three intensity levels:

Low  
Medium  
High

Every action must have:

- cooldown
- positive effect
- potential negative side effect

---

# 7. Event Frequency

During daytime:

One event every

30–90 real minutes

Events must use deterministic weighted selection.

---

# 8. Event Categories

Event diversity must include:

Water stress  
Nutrient issues  
Pests  
Disease / mold risk  
Environmental stress  
Growth boosts

---

# 9. End Result Logic

The final plant quality depends on:

Average health during lifecycle  
Stress accumulation  
Player decision quality

Possible outcomes:

Perfect harvest  
Good harvest  
Average harvest  
Damaged harvest  
Failed plant

---

# 10. Design Philosophy

The simulation should reward:

- consistent care
- correct timing
- risk management

Players should learn plant biology through gameplay.
